The Health Technology Assessment (HTA) European Special Interest Group, sponsored by PSI and EFSPI, is a fast-growing community of around 100 professionals working in HTA statistics. Our SIG provides a platform for connecting people across companies and geographies to discuss industry developments, organize events, and advance scientific knowledge and best practices in HTA statistics.

We meet monthly to:

  • Share recent developments in HTA statistics
  • Discuss how to advance the objectives of the HTA SIG
  • Collaborate on specialized HTA statistics topics through our subteams

What is HTA statistics?

HTA statistics is the application of statistics to Health Technology Assessment: the scientific process that helps authorities decide medical technologies and treatments are effective and worth paying for.

HTA statisticians are the "swiss army knives" among pharma statisticians - they handle a broad range of topics, from analysis of clinical trial data and (network) meta analysis to health economic modelling.

With the evolving policy landscape, in particular pan-European HTA, there's a growing need for statisticians who can successfully navigate the unique context of HTA.
